About Nature's Drop Organic Oils

The love of a mother in it’s purest form reflected in the face of their little one results in seeing their baby as the cutest in the whole world. But when guests visit to welcome your new born and go. “ Good Lord what’s wrong with her skin? Your heart drops, even worse when you watch your little angel so helplessly scratching that dry flaky skin because the itch is too much then crying because the scratching broke the skin even more, there’s a pain you feel. It is that pain and desperation coupled with the disapointment from products that just won’t work that gave birth to Nature’s drop organic oils. It was never meant to be a business,just avocado oil made at home by a desperate mom to help ease her baby girls struggle with eczema. But greatness can come from adversity. By tapping into what mother nature so generously gives, we hope to bring good health not just to our children but to all humanity. We don’t just offer organic oils we make them with the same mother’s love the first drop was made with. But remember what’s good for baby is definitely good for you.
